buycycle assistant

    Main features v0.1:

    • FAQ Support: Directs users to relevant FAQ sections for quick, helpful information.
    • Buyers guide: Assist you in finding the ideal bicycle, we will guide you through a series of informed questions, allowing us to offer tailored bike suggestions that best suit your requirements.

    Planned features:

    • FAQ Support: Use faq articles as basis for answering questions instead of referencing to them.
    • Thumbs up/down for feedback

    Feedback and Issue Reporting:

    Report any issues or suggestions to sebastian@buycycle.com or through our Slack channel, using this format:

    user_id: [Your Name], Datetime: [Date and Time], Issue: [Specific issue or suggestion]. e.g: user_id: Sebastian, Datetime: 3/7/2024, 12:01:16 PM, Issue: Recommendations not relevant.

    To checkout chatbot's code, visit our GitHub repo: https://github.com/buycycle/assistant.
